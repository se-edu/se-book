    Quality of Requirements

    Here are some characteristics of well-defined requirements [ðŸ“– zielczynski]:

    • Unambiguous
    • Testable (verifiable)
    • Clear (concise, terse, simple, precise)
    • Correct
    • Understandable
    • Feasible (realistic, possible)
    • Independent
    • Atomic
    • Necessary
